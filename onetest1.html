FAQS
Can I still redeem the Fortnite Bhangra Boogie Emote?

Yes, the Fortnite Bhangra Boogie Emote redemption is still open.

Who is eligible to redeem the Fornite Bhangra Boogie Emote?

Any customer who purchased a OnePlus 3, 3T, 5, 5T, 6, 6T, 6T McLaren, 7, 7 Pro, 7 Pro 5G, 7T, 7T Pro, 7T Pro 5G McLaren, 8 5G (T-Mobile), 8 5G UW (Verizon), 8, or 8 Pro.

I have an older OnePlus phone. Am I eligible for this offer?

No. The OnePlus One, OnePlus 2, & the OnePlus X do not qualify for this offer.

I have a Samsung/Apple device. Am I eligible for this offer?

No. The Fortnite Bhangra Boogie Emote is only offered for users with a OnePlus 3 or later device.

Do I need a OnePlus or Epic Games account to redeem my Fortnite Bhangra Boogie Emote?

You will need a OnePlus account (new or existing) and an Epic Games account (new or existing) to redeem your Fortnite Bhangra Boogie Emote. Each device will only receive one Emote redemption.

Can I redeem the Bhangra Boogie Emote more than once from my device?

No. Each device will only receive one Bhangra Boogie Emote. Once the Bhangra Boogie Emote is redeemed on a OnePlus device, it will not receive additional Emotes for different Fortnite accounts logging into the OnePlus device.

How do I redeem my Fortnite Bhangra Boogie Emote?

In order to redeem the Fortnite Bhangra Boogie Emote, first install Fortnite onto your device. Sign in to or sign up for an Epic Games account. Follow the instructions below to verify your OnePlus device & redeem the Bhangra Boogie Emote.

1. Sign into your OnePlus Account.

2. Find the IMEI number and PCBA number on your phone. To find your IMEI number on your device, open Phone Dialer and dial *#06#. To find your PCBA number, open Phone Dialer and dial *#888#. Verify them on the page. here.

3. After you receive the Fortnite Bhangra Boogie Emote redemption code via email, go to the Fortnite redemption page at: http://fortnite.com/redeem.

4. Your Bhangra Boogie Emote will be available in your Locker in Fortnite.*

*Only one Fortnite Bhangra Boogie Emote is granted per device.

How can I view my Fortnite Bhangra Boogie Emote?

From the in-game lobby, click the “Emote” button below the “Play” button near the bottom of the screen. This causes the Emote wheel to pop up. In the Emote Wheel you will see the Fortnite Bhangra Boogie Emote.

What if I am having issues accessing the Epic Games Launcher?

If you are receiving an error message or need help accessing the Epic Games Launcher, please go to https://epicgames.helpshift.com/a/epic-launcher/ for support and troubleshooting.

Does my Fortnite Bhangra Boogie Emote expire?

Your Bhangra Boogie Emote does not expire. Once you redeem the Bhangra Boogie Emote, it is yours to keep.

Is my Fortnite Bhangra Boogie Emote available for Fortnite on other consoles? (i.e. Xbox One, Play Station 4)

Your Fortnite Bhangra Boogie Emote will be available for use on any platform that you play on after redeeming it on your OnePlus device.

I have a Fortnite account already, can I use my same account on my new eligible OnePlus device?

Yes, when accessing the game for the first time you will be prompted to sign into your existing Epic Games account.

If I use my existing Fortnite account on my eligible OnePlus device, can I still access it on other consoles?

Yes, you can access the same Fortnite account on other platforms.

If I use my existing Fortnite account on my eligible OnePlus device, do I have access to my same purchased or earned content?

Yes, you will have access to any content that you have previously purchased or unlocked on that account.

Does Fortnite require a paid subscription for me to play?

No, Fortnite is free to play with no subscription required.

Can I access multiple Fortnite accounts on my OnePlus device? (i.e. I have a family member who wants to play)

Only one account may be signed in to Fortnite at any time. You can access multiple accounts on the same device but you will need to sign out of one account from the game menu before being able to access the other account.

What other resources are available if I have questions that are not answered by this document?

For Fortnite questions, please reference the Fortnite FAQ page or contact Fortnite support at fortnitehelp.epicgames.com
